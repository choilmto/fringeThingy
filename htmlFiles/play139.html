Scotch Tape

Company
For the Biscuit Theatre
Playwright/Creator
Marryl Smith
Runtime
50m

5 million subscribers. Over a billion views. Massive internet following. Dreamboat fiancé. Maggie’s entire life is online—and it looks perfect. But when you take the viewer out from behind the computer screen and into her filming process, some things can't be glossed over with editing. Maggie vows to share her whole life with her loyal fanbase, but with that comes some ugly truths that she can’t even admit to herself. In the age of the internet where likes equal currency and image is everything, how far will Maggie go to look like she has it all together?
